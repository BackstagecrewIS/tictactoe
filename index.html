How to play Noughts and Crosses

This is a two player game where players take turns to place their marker (O or X) into one of the nine squares on the board. The winner is the first player to place three markers on a line.

When a player wins, or the game is drawn, click the new game button to play again.

The game keeps score of each player's wins after each game.

The first turn is alternated between players to ensure fairness.

To start a new series of games, press the reset scores button. This will zero both win counters and start a new game
